Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh


Spiti Valley is a cold desert mountain valley located 412 km away from Shimla. There are many places to visit in Spiti Valley. Few of these are Komic Village, Langza Village, Lhalung Monastery, Tabo Caves, Tabo Monastery. Tabo Monastery is one of the oldest monasteries in the world and a favourite of the Dalai Lama.
